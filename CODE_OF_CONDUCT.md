# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

In our community spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on what is correct and useful, not personal characteristics.
- Acknowledge differing viewpoints and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable conduct includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or abusive language.
- Posting private or sensitive information.
- Threats, intimidation, or other harm directed at individuals or groups.

## Enforcement

If you believe someone is violating this Code of Conduct, please open an issue and include relevant context.

The maintainers may remove comments or content, close issues/PRs, or take other action they deem appropriate to protect the community.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by community best practices for open-source projects.
